HortiMED Project (Grant Number 1915) is part of the PRIMA Programme supported by the European Union's Horizon 2020 research and innovation programme. The HortiMED project is an action implemented by ,the National Institute of Oceanography and Fisheries (NIOF, Egypt, Coordinator), INKOA SISTEMAS S.L. (INKOA, Spain partner), the University Mohamed Khider, Biskra (UMKB, Algeria, partner) and Universidad de Deusto (UDeusto, Spain, partner).
HortiMED is a 60-month project focused on providing the Mediterranean horticultural community with innovative, cost-effective tools for resource-efficient, year-round greenhouse cultivation. This includes smart nutrient, irrigation, and climate control, plus integrated pest management.
